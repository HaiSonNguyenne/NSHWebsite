Abstract

This report summarizes and analyzes the Latent Dirichlet Allocation (LDA) model, a tool for uncovering hidden topics from large text corpora. We provide a brief overview of the theoretical foundations of the Dirichlet and multinomial distributions, as well as the probabilistic structure of LDA, including enhanced variants designed to improve efficiency in rare data scenarios.
